allogy.com SQRL is a new authentication mechanism that does not require this server to keep any secret information (like passwords). It is secure, quick, and reliable, but does require a special client program in order for it to work.

When using an untrusted computer, SQRL can be used directly from your mobile device (by scanning the qr code) to minimize the risk of an account takeover, or directly from your personal workstation (by clicking on the qr code).

It is not neccesary to create a new identity per-website, as the SQRL protocol automatically segregates your online identities. See the main sqrl page at grc.com for more information.

If allowed by the server, one can also *create* a new account by trying to login by this mechanism.
